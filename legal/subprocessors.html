Every third-party vendor that touches GradeEarn child data, what they do, what data they see, where they're located, and the status of our Data Processing Agreement with them.

What we do NOT use

To make the absence explicit (this is what protects kids):

  • No Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or other behavioral analytics
  • No Facebook SDK, TikTok SDK, or any social-network pixel
  • No advertising networks of any kind
  • No A/B testing service that uses persistent identifiers
  • No chat support widgets on child-facing screens
  • No biometric vendors (no voice prints, no face templates)

Changes

Before adding any new subprocessor that touches kid data, we will: (a) execute a DPA, (b) update this page and increment the Privacy Policy version, (c) email parents and require re-acceptance.
