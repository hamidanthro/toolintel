What Happens If Circumstances Change

If I ever acquire a financial interest in a company that could be reviewed:

  1. That company will be immediately excluded from review eligibility
  2. Any existing review will be flagged with a conflict notice
  3. The disclosure table above will be updated within 24 hours
  4. An explanation will be published on the Transparency page

Recusal Policy

I will not review any tool where:

  • I have a personal relationship with founders or executives
  • I have previously worked for or consulted with the company
  • I have any financial interest, however small
  • A family member has a material relationship with the company

In these cases, the tool will not be reviewed. No exceptions.

Challenge me on this. If you believe I have an undisclosed conflict of interest, email me at conflict@toolintel.ai. I will investigate and respond publicly within 7 days.
