About

Hi! I'm Saad, and I'm in 3rd grade in Texas.

I built this website because when I wanted to practice for my STAAR test, I noticed something annoying — there was no good place to find math questions made just for my grade, my state, and the way the STAAR test actually looks. The practice questions were scattered all over the internet, and most sites just give you the same problems over and over again.

So I made something different. My site uses real AI — not just a long list of questions someone typed up a long time ago. The AI pays attention to you. If a problem is too easy, it gives you a harder one. If you get stuck, it explains the answer step by step. If something is way too tricky, it helps you slow down and figure it out. It learns what kind of math you need to work on most.

A fun fact about me: I build AI-powered apps, and I'm only 9 years old.

I want every kid who uses this site to feel smart, strong, and ready for STAAR. And I'd really love it if you leave reviews, questions, and ideas to make the site better — together we can build the biggest, friendliest math community for Texas kids.

Let's get good at math together!
