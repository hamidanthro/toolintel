How StarTest works

A friendly tour of every part of the app — from grade-aligned questions to earning points and shipping real toys home.

📚

Grade-specific practice

Questions for Kindergarten through Grade 8 plus high school courses, written to match your state's academic standards and the format of your state's actual test — whether that's STAAR in Texas, TCAP in Tennessee, FSA in Florida, or any other state assessment.

  • Pick your state and grade — every question is aligned to the standards your child is actually tested on.
  • Tens of thousands of unique questions per grade and subject, generated and validated for correctness.
  • The app remembers which questions you've already answered so you never see the same one twice — until you've mastered the bank.
🤖

An AI tutor that adapts

When you miss a question, an AI tutor steps in. It asks you what you tried, walks you through the right way to think about it, and never just hands over the answer.

  • Step-by-step hints — not just a correct/incorrect mark.
  • Adjusts explanations to your grade level and the way you answered.
  • Learns the topics you find tricky and surfaces them more.
🪙

Earn points for every correct answer

Right answers earn you points (we call them "cents") that go straight into your wallet. Harder questions and longer streaks earn more.

  • Sign in to start earning — guests can practice 100 questions for free first.
  • There's a lifetime cap so things stay fair for everyone.
  • Wallet syncs to the cloud, so your points follow you to any device.
🏆

Leaderboard & streaks

Climb the live leaderboard as you answer more questions correctly. Your home dashboard shows the top 10, your rank, and how your accuracy compares.

  • Updates every 30 seconds — no need to refresh.
  • Ranked by total correct answers, with accuracy as the tiebreaker.
  • Daily streaks and best-streak badges keep practice fun.
  • Compete nationally or filter by your state and grade.
🎁

Spend points on real toys

Open the Toy Marketplace to see what's available. When you have enough points, pick a toy and check out — we ship it to your door, anywhere in the U.S.

  • Browse anytime, even before signing up.
  • Each toy shows its price in points, stock level, and a clear "you can afford this" badge once you do.
  • Orders show up in My orders so you can track shipping status.
👨‍👩‍👧

Parents are always in the loop

Toys are real. Shipping is real. So a grown-up has to approve every order before it leaves our system.

  • Checkout asks for the parent's name, email, phone, and shipping address.
  • A required consent box confirms the adult is the parent or legal guardian.
  • Points have no cash value outside this app — they only redeem for the toys we list.
📦

Shipping & delivery

Once a parent approves an order, our team gets to work.

  • Status updates flow through pending → approved → shipped → delivered.
  • Tracking numbers appear in your orders panel as soon as a label prints.
  • Shipping is included in the point price — no surprise fees. We ship to all 50 states.
🔒

Safe by design

We collect only what's needed to make the app work and to ship the toy.

  • No ads. No third-party trackers.
  • Kids' usernames are never shared outside the leaderboard, where only display names appear.
  • Parent contact info is used to confirm orders and ship — nothing else.
