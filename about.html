About STAAR Prep

STAAR Prep is a free study tool aligned to the Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills (TEKS) for math. It covers the State of Texas Assessments of Academic Readiness (STAAR) for grades 3–8 and the Algebra I End-of-Course exam.

This site is independent and not affiliated with or endorsed by the Texas Education Agency.
