🧱 How to play
  1. Blocks fall with random digits on every cell.
  2. A row clears when the 10 digits sum to your TARGET (shown at top).

  4. Desktop: ← → move · ↑ rotate · ↓ soft drop · Space hard drop · P pause.
  5. Earn cents! 1 row = 1¢, Tetris (4 rows) = 5¢. Daily cap: 50¢.
