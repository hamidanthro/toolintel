Deep Dive Analysis

Core AI Performance ‚Äî 74/100 ‚Üì

ReplyQuik handles straightforward FAQ-style questions well. In our 2-week test across 150 sample queries:

  • Simple FAQs: 92% accurate (hours, pricing, location)
  • Product questions: 78% accurate
