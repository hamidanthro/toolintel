ðŸ’¡ What the marketing doesn't tell you

1. "8x7B" doesn't mean 56B parameters active. The Mixture of Experts architecture only activates 12.9B parameters per token. You get 70B-class performance, but the model size is closer to 13B in actual compute. Marketing loves to emphasize "8x7B" to sound bigger.

2. Memory requirements are brutal. Despite sparse activation, you still need to load ALL 46.7B parameters into VRAM. Budget for 2-4x A100 (80GB) GPUs minimum. Cloud costs for serving Mixtral run $3-6/hr continuous.

3. Mistral AI is NOT fully transparent. Despite "open weights," training data composition, filtering methods, and hyperparameters are undisclosed. You can use it, fine-tune it, but you can't truly replicate it. "Open" has limits.

What We Tested

  • Model: Mixtral-8x7B-v0.1 (base model)
  • Infrastructure: 2x A100 (80GB) on AWS p4d.24xlarge

  • Inference speed: 15-25 tokens/sec (vLLM, 2x A100)
  • Handles diverse tasks well (benefit of MoE specialization)
  • Strong multilingual capabilities (trained on diverse data)
