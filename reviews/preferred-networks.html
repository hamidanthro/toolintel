The Full Stack Advantage

PFN's vertical integration spans four layers:

1. AI Chips

Custom silicon designed in-house for AI workloads. Optimized for their specific model architectures and use cases.

2. Computing Infrastructure

Proprietary infrastructure built on their chips. Offers on-premise deployment for data-sensitive industries.

3. Foundation Models

PLaMo (Preferred Language Model) and other models trained for Japanese language and domain-specific tasks.

4. Applications

Industry-specific solutions for manufacturing, healthcare, transportation, and other sectors.

The Trade-off: This integration offers performance and control but creates dependence on a single vendor's ecosystem.
