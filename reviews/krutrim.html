Real-World Testing Results

What we tested: Hindi chatbot, English content generation, basic coding assistance, regional language translation.

Performance:

  • Hindi: Acceptable for simple queries, struggles with complex topics
  • English: Below GPT-3.5 quality, factual errors common
  • Coding: Poor; generates broken code, limited language support
  • Regional languages: Very limited; often produces gibberish

Reliability: API downtime encountered twice during 7-day testing period.

The Ola Factor

  • Strength: Ola's distribution network, India market knowledge
  • Weakness: Limited AI research track record, rushed launch
  • Strategy: India-first positioning, but quality needs significant improvement
