üí° What the marketing page doesn't tell you

1. You can't just sign up. Baichuan requires enterprise verification and sales approval. Individual developers are effectively locked out.

2. Domain models are the real product. The general Baichuan-13B is decent but unremarkable. The specialized medical/legal variants are where the value lies.

3. "Open-source" comes with strings. While base models are technically open, commercial use requires licensing agreements. Not truly FOSS.
