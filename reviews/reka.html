Pricing Analysis

Public Information: Limited. Pricing not published on main site as of Feb 2026.

API Access: Available through developer portal, but tier structure and costs require account signup.

Enterprise: Custom pricing, requires sales contact.

üí∞ What buyers get wrong about Reka pricing

Hidden pricing = budget risk. Without transparent pricing, you can't accurately budget. This works for large enterprises with procurement teams, not startups.

Edge deployment isn't "free." Running models on-device saves API costs but adds engineering cost for optimization, testing, and maintenance.

Value Assessment: For teams building production multimodal applications with budget for enterprise contracts, Reka offers strong capabilities. For smaller teams or hobbyists, lack of transparent pricing is a barrier.
