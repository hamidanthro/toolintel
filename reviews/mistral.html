üí° What the marketing page doesn't tell you

1. "Open source" isn't quite open source. Mistral uses the term "open weights" ‚Äî you can download and run the models, but training code and datasets aren't public. This is transparency, but not full open source.

2. European hosting comes at a premium. EU-region API endpoints cost 15-20% more than US regions. If data sovereignty is why you're here, budget accordingly.

3. The platform is developer-first, not user-friendly. Unlike ChatGPT or Claude, there's no polished consumer interface. You're expected to use APIs or build your own front-end.

üá™üá∫ European Enterprise

What we used it for: Multilingual customer support, document processing in French/German, GDPR-compliant data analysis.

What worked: Exceptional multilingual performance. French responses were more natural than US models. GDPR compliance made procurement easy. Self-hosting option satisfied legal team.

What didn't: Smaller context window limited document processing. Had to chunk large contracts. No native integrations with Salesforce or common EU tools.
