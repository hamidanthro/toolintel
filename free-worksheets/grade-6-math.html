  3. 1435.069
  4. 1435.080

Why: Multiply as whole numbers (2158 × 665 = 1,435,070), then place 3 decimal places: 1435.070.

Ratios, Rates & Proportional Reasoning

TEKS 6.4B,6.4C,6.4D,6.4E,6.5A

Use ratios, rates, unit rates, and percents to solve proportional problems.

1

Complete the equivalent ratio: 11/10 = 88/?.

  1. 81
  2. 82
  3. 79
  4. 80 ✓

Why: Multiply both sides by 8: 11:10 = 88:80.

2

6:8 = 24:?. Find the missing number.

  1. 31
  2. 34
  3. 32 ✓
  4. 33

Why: Multiply both sides by 4: 6:8 = 24:32.

3

143 puzzle pieces are split equally among 11 students. Each student gets how many puzzle pieces?

  1. 13 ✓
  2. 12
  3. 15
  4. 14

Why: 143 ÷ 11 = 13.

4

75% of 16 = ?

  1. 13
  2. 14
  3. 12 ✓
  4. 11

Why: 75% means 75/100. 75/100 × 16 = 12.

5

An item costs $160. Sales tax is 20%. What is the total cost?

  1. $192 ✓
  2. $191
  3. $193
  4. $197

Why: Tax: 20% of $160 = $32.00. Total: $160 + $32.00 = $192.00.

Expressions, Equations & Inequalities

TEKS 6.7A,6.7B,6.7C,6.7D,6.9A,6.10A

Evaluate expressions, generate equivalent forms, and solve one-step equations and inequalities.

1

Evaluate 9x² - 2 when x = 4.

  1. 141
  2. 144
  3. 142 ✓
  4. 143

Why: 9(4)² − 2 = 9·16 − 2 = 142.

2

Evaluate 9x² - 11 when x = 7.

  1. 429
  2. 432
  3. 431
  4. 430 ✓

Why: 9(7)² − 11 = 9·49 − 11 = 430.

3

Simplify: 4x + 5 - 4x - 9

  1. -3
  2. -2
  3. -4 ✓
  4. -5

Why: Combine like terms: x-terms 4x - 4x = 0; constants 5 - 9 = -4.

4

Solve for x: x − 28 = -23

  1. 7
  2. 5 ✓
  3. 6
  4. 4

Why: Add 28 to both sides: x = -23 + 28 = 5.

5

Which value of x makes the inequality true? x + 4 ≥ 27

  1. 21
  2. 23 ✓
  3. 20
  4. 22

Why: Subtract 4: x ≥ 23. The true choice is 23.

Geometry & Measurement

Compute area of triangles, parallelograms, trapezoids; volume of right rectangular prisms; coordinate plane.

1

A parallelogram has base 13 and height 21. Find its area.

  1. 274
  2. 273 ✓
  3. 275
  4. 272

Why: A = b × h = 13 × 21 = 273.

2

Find the area of a triangle with base 26 units and height 20 units.

  1. 261
  2. 259
  3. 262
  4. 260 ✓

Why: A = ½ × b × h = ½ × 26 × 20 = 260.

3

A trapezoid has bases 5 and 16 and height 2. Find its area.

  1. 22
  2. 23
  3. 20
  4. 21 ✓

Why: A = ½(b₁ + b₂)h = ½(5 + 16)(2) = 21.

4

  3. 1532
  4. 1531

Why: V = l × w × h = 17 × 15 × 6 = 1,530 cubic units.

5

What is the volume of a 4 by 10 by 3 box?

  1. 122
  2. 120 ✓
  3. 121
  4. 119

Why: V = l × w × h = 4 × 10 × 3 = 120 cubic units.
