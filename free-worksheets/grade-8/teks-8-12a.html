Imani is organizing a fundraiser for her school and needs to buy 3 boxes of cookies. Each box costs $4.50. If she pays with a $20 bill, how much change will she receive after buying the boxes of cookies?

  1. $6.50 ✓
  2. $7.50
  3. $8.50
  4. $9.50

Why: First, calculate the total cost of the cookies: 3 boxes * $4.50 per box = $13.50. Then, subtract this total from the amount Imani paid: $20 - $13.50 = $6.50. Therefore, Imani will receive $6.50 in change.

Chen wants to buy supplies for a science project. He finds a pack of markers that costs $5.75, a notebook for $3.50, and a set of colored pencils for $4.25. If Chen has $20, how much money will he have left after buying all three items?

  1. $6.50 ✓
  2. $6.00
  3. $5.75
  4. $7.25

Why: To find out how much money Chen will have left, first add the costs of the items: $5.75 + $3.50 + $4.25 = $13.50. Next, subtract this total from the amount Chen has: $20 - $13.50 = $6.50. Therefore, Chen will have $6.50 left after buying all the supplies.

3

Aisha is planning a birthday party and wants to buy balloons. A pack of 12 balloons costs $7.50. If Aisha wants to buy 4 packs of balloons, how much will she spend in total?

  1. $30.00 ✓
  2. $25.00
  3. $27.50
  4. $35.00

Why: To find the total cost for 4 packs of balloons, first multiply the cost of one pack by the number of packs: $7.50 * 4 = $30.00. Therefore, Aisha will spend $30.00 in total.
