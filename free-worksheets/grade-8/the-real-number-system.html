√10 is between which pair of integers?

  1. 2 and 3
  2. 9 and 16
  3. 4 and 5
  4. 3 and 4 ✓

Why: 3² = 9 and 4² = 16. Since 9 < 10 < 16, √10 is between 3 and 4.
