1

Mateo is saving money to buy a new bicycle that costs $300. He currently has $120 saved and plans to save an additional $40 each month. Which equation best models the total amount of money Mateo will have saved after x months?

  1. y = 40x + 120 ✓
  2. y = 300 - 40x
  3. y = 120 - 40x
  4. y = 120 + 300x

Why: Mateo's total savings after x months consists of his initial amount of $120 plus $40 for each month he saves. The equation that models this scenario is y = 40x + 120, where y represents his total savings after x months.

2

Aisha is saving money to buy a new laptop. She currently has $75 saved and plans to save an additional $25 each week. Which of the following equations best represents the total amount of money Aisha will have after w weeks?

  1. T = 75 + 25w ✓
  2. T = 25 - 75w
  3. T = 75 - 25w
  4. T = 75 * 25w

Why: The correct equation is T = 75 + 25w. This represents that Aisha starts with $75 and adds $25 for each week she saves. Therefore, after w weeks, her total savings will be the initial amount plus the amount saved each week, making the total T equal to 75 plus 25 times the number of weeks (w).

3

Priya is saving money to buy a new bicycle. She has already saved $150 and plans to save an additional $20 each week. Which expression best represents the total amount of money Priya will have saved after w weeks?

  1. 150 + 20w ✓
  2. 20 + w
  3. 150w
  4. 150 - 20w

Why: The expression that represents the total amount of money Priya will have saved after w weeks is 150 + 20w. Here, 150 is the amount she has already saved, and 20w represents the additional savings of $20 each week, with w being the number of weeks.

4

Amara is saving money to buy a new laptop that costs $300. She starts with $120 and saves an additional $30 each week. Which expression represents the total amount of money Amara will have after 'w' weeks?

  1. 120 + 30w ✓
  2. 30w - 120
  3. 300 - 30w
  4. 120 - 30w

Why: The correct expression is 120 + 30w, as it shows that Amara starts with $120 and adds $30 for each week she saves. Therefore, after 'w' weeks, her total savings can be represented by that expression.
