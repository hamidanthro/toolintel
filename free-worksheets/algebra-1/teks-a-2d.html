Mateo is planning a birthday party and wants to buy party bags for his guests. Each party bag costs $3. If Mateo invites 10 friends to his party, how much will he spend on the party bags in total?

  1. $30 ✓
  2. $20
  3. $15
  4. $25

Why: To find the total cost of the party bags, multiply the cost of each bag by the number of friends Mateo is inviting. So, the calculation is 3 * 10 = 30. Therefore, Mateo will spend $30 on the party bags.

  1. $200 ✓
  2. $180

  4. $250

Why: To find out how much money Amara makes, multiply the number of boxes sold by the price per box. So, 40 boxes * $5/box = $200. Therefore, the correct answer is $200.

Aisha is selling homemade cookies at a bake sale. She sells each cookie for $3. If she sells 20 cookies, which expression represents the total revenue she makes from the sales?

  1. 3 * 20 ✓
  2. 3 + 20
  3. 20 - 3
  4. 20 / 3

Why: The correct expression that represents the total revenue Aisha makes from selling 20 cookies at $3 each is 3 * 20, which equals $60. The other choices do not correctly calculate total revenue: 3 + 20 gives a total of 23, which is not revenue; 20 - 3 equals 17, which doesn't apply here; and 20 / 3 is a division that does not represent revenue from cookie sales.
