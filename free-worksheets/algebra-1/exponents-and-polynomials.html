Simplify: (x^6)^2. What is the exponent of x?

  1. 13
  2. 14
  3. 11
  4. 12 ✓

Why: When raising a power to a power, multiply exponents: 6 × 2 = 12.

2

Simplify: (x^4)^3. What is the exponent of x?

  1. 12 ✓
  2. 11
  3. 13
  4. 14

Why: When raising a power to a power, multiply exponents: 4 × 3 = 12.

3

Expand (x + 5)(x + 6). What is the coefficient of x in the expanded form?

  1. 11 ✓
  2. 13
  3. 10
  4. 12

Why: Use FOIL: x·x + x·6 + 5·x + 5·6 = x² + 11x + 30. The coefficient of x is 11.

4

Expand (x − 5)(x − 7). What is the coefficient of x in the expanded form?

  1. -13
  2. -10
  3. -12 ✓
  4. -11

Why: Use FOIL: x·x + x·-7 + -5·x + -5·-7 = x² + -12x + 35. The coefficient of x is -12.

5

Expand (x + 4)(x − 3). What is the coefficient of x in the expanded form?

  1. 2
  2. 0
  3. 3
  4. 1 ✓

Why: Use FOIL: x·x + x·-3 + 4·x + 4·-3 = x² + 1x + -12. The coefficient of x is 1.

6

Expand (x − 7)(x + 4). What is the coefficient of x in the expanded form?

  1. -2
  2. -1
  3. -4
  4. -3 ✓

Why: Use FOIL: x·x + x·4 + -7·x + -7·4 = x² + -3x + -28. The coefficient of x is -3.

7

Factor: 7x² + 28x

  1. 7(x² + 4x)
  2. x(7x + 28)
  3. 7x(x + 4) ✓
  4. 7x(7x + 4)

Why: The GCF of 7x² and 28x is 7x. Factoring out: 7x(x + 4).

8

Factor: x² − 1x − 56

  1. (x − 8)(x + 7) ✓
  2. (x − 8)(x − 7)
  3. (x + 8)(x − 7)
  4. (x + 8)(x + 7)

Why: Find two numbers whose product is -56 and sum is -1: -8 and 7. So x² − 1x − 56 = (x − 8)(x + 7).
