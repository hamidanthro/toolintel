Lila is organizing a bake sale to raise money for her school. She wants to sell cupcakes and cookies. If she sells each cupcake for $2 and each cookie for $1, which of the following represents her total earnings if she sells c cupcakes and k cookies?

  1. 2c + k ✓
  2. c + 2k
  3. 2c + 2k
  4. c + k

Why: The total earnings from selling cupcakes and cookies can be calculated by multiplying the number of cupcakes (c) by the price of each cupcake ($2) and adding that to the number of cookies (k) multiplied by the price of each cookie ($1). Therefore, the correct expression for total earnings is 2c + k.
