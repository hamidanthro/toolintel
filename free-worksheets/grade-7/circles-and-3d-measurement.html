A triangular prism has a triangle base (b=8, h=4) and length 12. What is its volume?

  1. 193
  2. 191
  3. 194
  4. 192 ✓

Why: Base area B = ½ × 8 × 4 = 16. V = B × length = 16 × 12 = 192.
