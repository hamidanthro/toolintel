Fractions

  1. 4
  2. 3 ✓
  3. 5
  4. 2

2

Sofia ate 2 of 8 equal slices of pecan pie. What fraction did she eat?

  1. 1/8
  2. 2/8 ✓
  3. 8/2

3

4

  2. 1/4
  3. 1/3 ✓
  4. 2/3

  1. 1
  2. 2 ✓
  3. 3
  4. 6

Why: 6 ÷ 3 = 2 flowers each.

Money

TEKS 2.5A

Find the total value of a set of coins.

1

What is the total value of 3 pennys and 2 quarters?

  1. 53 ✓
  2. 54
  3. 52
  4. 55

Why: 3 × 1¢ = 3¢; 2 × 25¢ = 50¢. Total = 53¢.

2

Sebastian has 4 pennys and 2 nickels. How many cents in all?

  1. 15
  2. 13
  3. 16
  4. 14 ✓

Why: 4 × 1¢ = 4¢; 2 × 5¢ = 10¢. Total = 14¢.

3

Find the total: 1 dime and 2 quarters and 1 penny.

  1. 61 ✓
  2. 62
  3. 60
  4. 63

Why: 1 × 10¢ = 10¢; 2 × 25¢ = 50¢; 1 × 1¢ = 1¢. Total = 61¢.

4

Wyatt has 3 pennys and 3 quarters and 3 nickels. How many cents in all?

  1. 93 ✓
  2. 95
  3. 92
  4. 94

Why: 3 × 1¢ = 3¢; 3 × 25¢ = 75¢; 3 × 5¢ = 15¢. Total = 93¢.

5

Isabella has 3 nickels and 1 dime and 3 pennys. How many cents in all?

  1. 27
  2. 30
  3. 29
  4. 28 ✓

Why: 3 × 5¢ = 15¢; 1 × 10¢ = 10¢; 3 × 1¢ = 3¢. Total = 28¢.
