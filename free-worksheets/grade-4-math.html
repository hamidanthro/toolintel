Fractions: Equivalence & Comparison

TEKS 4.3A,4.3C,4.3D,4.3G

Build, compare, and decompose fractions and mixed numbers.

1

Which fraction is equivalent to 3/4?

  1. 6/12
  2. 9/12 ✓
  3. 12/20
  4. 15/24

Why: Multiply numerator and denominator by 3: 3×3/4×3 = 9/12.

2

Which fraction is greater than 2/3?

  1. 1/2
  2. 3/5
  3. 5/8
  4. 3/4 ✓

Why: 2/3 ≈ 0.667 and 3/4 = 0.75, so 3/4 > 2/3.

3

Which sum equals 5/8?

  1. 1/8 + 1/8 + 1/8
  2. 2/8 + 2/8
  3. 3/8 + 2/8 ✓
  4. 1/4 + 1/4

Why: 3/8 + 2/8 = 5/8. The other options sum to 3/8, 4/8, and 2/4.

  1. 24/16
  2. 6/32
  3. 7/16
  4. 6/16 ✓

Why: Same denominator: subtract numerators. 15 − 9 = 6, so the answer is 6/16.
