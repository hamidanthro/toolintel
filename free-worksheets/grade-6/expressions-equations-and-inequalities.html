  1. 74 ✓

Simplify: 8x + 9 - 1x - 12

  1. 6x - 3
  2. 8x - 3
  3. 7x - 3 ✓
  4. 7x - 2

Why: Combine like terms: x-terms 8x - 1x = 7x; constants 9 - 12 = -3.

5

Simplify: 8x + 5 - 9x - 4

  1. -x + 1 ✓
  2. -2x + 1
  3. -x + 2
  4. 1

Why: Combine like terms: x-terms 8x - 9x = -x; constants 5 - 4 = 1.

6

Solve for x: x ÷ 9 = 4

  1. 35
  2. 38
  3. 37
  4. 36 ✓

Why: Multiply both sides by 9: x = 9 × 4 = 36.

7

Solve for x: x − 5 = 10

  1. 17
  2. 15 ✓
  3. 16
  4. 14

Why: Add 5 to both sides: x = 10 + 5 = 15.

Which value of x makes the inequality true? x + 3 ≤ 11

  1. 10
  2. 11
  3. 9
  4. 8 ✓

Why: Subtract 3: x ≤ 8. The true choice is 8.
