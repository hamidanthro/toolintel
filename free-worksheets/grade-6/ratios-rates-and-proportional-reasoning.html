Complete the equivalent ratio: 11/10 = 88/?.

  1. 81
  2. 82
  3. 79
  4. 80 ✓

Why: Multiply both sides by 8: 11:10 = 88:80.

2

Noah mixes dolls and index cards in the ratio 11:11. If there are 88 index cards, how many dolls?

  1. 88 ✓
  2. 87
  3. 90
  4. 89

Why: Multiply both sides by 8: 11:11 = 88:88.

3

Levi mixes erasers and marbles in the ratio 3:4. If there are 16 marbles, how many erasers?

  1. 14
  2. 12 ✓
  3. 13
  4. 11

Why: Multiply both sides by 4: 3:4 = 12:16.

4

27 oranges are split equally among 3 students. Each student gets how many oranges?

  1. 10
  2. 11
  3. 9 ✓
  4. 8

Why: 27 ÷ 3 = 9.

5

Ava packs 66 grapes evenly into 11 boxes. grapes per box?

  1. 5
  2. 8
  3. 7
  4. 6 ✓

Why: Unit rate: 66 ÷ 11 = 6 grapes per group.

6

5% of 440 = ?

  1. 24
  2. 22 ✓
  3. 23
  4. 21

Why: 5% means 5/100. 5/100 × 440 = 22.

7

What is 100% of 46?

  1. 45
  2. 48
  3. 46 ✓
  4. 47

Why: 100% means 100/100. 100/100 × 46 = 46.

8

An item costs $84. Sales tax is 15%. What is the total cost?

  1. $97.60
  2. $96.60 ✓
  3. $101.60
  4. $95.60

Why: Tax: 15% of $84 = $12.60. Total: $84 + $12.60 = $96.60.
