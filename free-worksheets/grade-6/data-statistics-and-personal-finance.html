Find the mean of this data set: 24, 30, 5, 6, 7, 30.

  1. 17 ✓
  2. 18
  3. 16
  4. 19

Why: Mean = sum ÷ count = 102 ÷ 6 = 17.

2

Find the range of this data set: 5, 25, 15, 19, 8, 7.

  1. 21
  2. 20 ✓
  3. 22
  4. 19

Why: Range = max − min = 25 − 5 = 20.

3

Find the median of this data set: 18, 5, 15, 20, 13.

  1. 16
  2. 17
  3. 14
  4. 15 ✓

Why: Sorted: 5, 13, 15, 18, 20. The middle value is 15.

4

Find the median of this data set: 2, 19, 16, 15, 29, 17.

  1. 19
  2. 17 ✓
  3. 18
  4. 16

Why: Sorted: 2, 15, 16, 17, 19, 29. The middle value is 17.

5

Find the range of this data set: 20, 1, 26, 19, 13, 3, 29.

  1. 29
  2. 30
  3. 28 ✓
  4. 27

Why: Range = max − min = 29 − 1 = 28.

6

Lily deposits $2100 at 3% simple interest per year. How much interest after 6 years?

  1. 377
  2. 380
  3. 378 ✓
  4. 379

Why: I = P × r × t = 2100 × 3/100 × 6 = 378.

  1. 2100 ✓
  2. 2099
  3. 2101
  4. 2102

Why: I = P × r × t = 4200 × 10/100 × 5 = 2100.
