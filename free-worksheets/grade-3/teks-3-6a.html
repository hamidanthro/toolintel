Camila went to a Texas farmers' market and bought 3 apples, each costing $2, and 2 oranges, each costing $3. How much money did Camila spend in total?

  1. $12 ✓
  2. $9
  3. $8
  4. $10

Why: To find the total amount Camila spent, we first calculate the cost of the apples: 3 apples × $2 = $6. Then, we calculate the cost of the oranges: 2 oranges × $3 = $6. Finally, we add the cost of the apples and oranges together: $6 + $6 = $12. Therefore, Camila spent $12 in total.

Valentina is visiting Palo Duro Canyon and wants to classify the different shapes she sees. She sees a cone-shaped rock formation and a cylinder-shaped water tower. What is the name of the shape that describes the rock formation?

  1. cone ✓
  2. cylinder
  3. sphere
  4. cube

Why: The rock formation that Valentina sees is shaped like a cone, which has a circular base and a pointed top. The cylinder-shaped water tower has straight sides and a circular base, but the correct answer for the rock formation is cone.

Aaliyah is building a model of a lighthouse using different shapes. She has a cone for the top, a cylinder for the body, and a rectangular prism for the base. Which solid figure is being used for the body of the lighthouse?

  1. cylinder ✓
  2. cube
  3. sphere
  4. cone

Why: The question asks which solid Aaliyah is using for the body of the lighthouse. The body is made with a cylinder, which is a three-dimensional shape with circular bases. The other options are incorrect because a cube has square faces, a sphere is round all over, and a cone has a pointed top. Therefore, the correct answer is cylinder.

Ella has a collection of geometric shapes that she is using to create art for the State Fair of Texas. She has 4 cubes, 3 spheres, and 5 cones. What is the total number of shapes that Ella has?

  1. 12 ✓
  2. 11
  3. 10
  4. 8

Why: To find the total number of shapes that Ella has, we need to add the number of cubes, spheres, and cones together. Ella has 4 cubes + 3 spheres + 5 cones, which equals 12 shapes in total.

Antonio is at the San Antonio Stock Show and Rodeo. He buys 25 tacos from a vendor. Later, he eats 12 tacos with his friends. How many tacos does Antonio have left?

  1. 13 ✓
  2. 12
  3. 15
  4. 10

Why: To find out how many tacos Antonio has left, we start with the 25 tacos he bought and then subtract the 12 tacos he ate. So, 25 - 12 = 13. Therefore, Antonio has 13 tacos left.

  1. rectangle ✓
  2. triangle
  3. cylinder
  4. sphere

Why: The base of Janelle's building is a rectangular prism, which has a rectangle as its base. Therefore, the correct choice is rectangle.

Elijah is making a cactus garden in his backyard in Texas. He has 3 rectangular planters that are each 2 feet long and 1 foot wide. What is the total area of Elijah's planters in square feet?

  1. 6 square feet ✓
  2. 5 square feet
  3. 7 square feet
  4. 4 square feet

Why: To find the total area of the planters, we first find the area of one rectangular planter by multiplying its length (2 feet) by its width (1 foot). So, the area of one planter is 2 * 1 = 2 square feet. Since Elijah has 3 identical planters, we multiply the area of one planter by 3: 2 * 3 = 6 square feet. Therefore, the total area of Elijah's planters is 6 square feet.

  1. 2
  2. 3 ✓
  3. 4
  4. 6

Why: To find out how many soccer balls each team gets, you need to divide the total number of soccer balls (18) by the number of teams (6). So, 18 ÷ 6 = 3. Each team will receive 3 soccer balls.
