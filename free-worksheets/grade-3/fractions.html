A pizza is cut into 8 equal slices. Maya eats 3 slices. What fraction did she eat?

  1. 3/5
  2. 3/8 ✓
  3. 5/8
  4. 8/3

Why: The whole has 8 equal parts (denominator). Maya ate 3 of them (numerator). So she ate 3/8.

2

Which of these equals 5/8?

  1. 6/40
  2. 25/40 ✓
  3. 5/40
  4. 10/13

Why: Multiply numerator and denominator by 5: 5×5 = 25, 8×5 = 40. So 5/8 = 25/40.

Why: Same denominator means compare numerators: 1 < 2, so 1/3 < 2/3.
