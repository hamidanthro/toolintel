Which number has a 7 in the thousands place?

  1. 27,041
  2. 70,402
  3. 12,704
  4. 47,000 ✓

Why: In 47,000, the digit 7 is in the thousands place. The 4 is in the ten-thousands place.

In the number 5,994,243, what is the value of the 9?

  1. 90000 ✓
  2. 89999
  3. 90002
  4. 90001

Why: The digit 9 sits in the ten-thousands place, so its value is 9 × 10,000 = 90,000.

3

934 — what value does the 4 have?

  1. 4 ✓
  2. 3
  3. 6
  4. 5

Why: The digit 4 sits in the ones place, so its value is 4 × 1 = 4.
