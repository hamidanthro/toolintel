Mika has a collection of Texas wildlife figurines. She has 2,345 figurines in total. If 1,576 of them are animals that can be found in the Big Bend area, how many figurines does Mika have that are not Big Bend animals?

  1. 769 ✓
  2. 868
  3. 1,768
  4. 2,576

Why: To find the number of figurines that Mika has which are not Big Bend animals, subtract the number of Big Bend animals from the total number of figurines. That is, 2,345 - 1,576 = 769. Therefore, Mika has 769 figurines that are not Big Bend animals.

Jamal collected 3,275 pinecones during a nature walk in Lufkin, Texas. He decided to give 1,468 pinecones to his friends for a craft project. How many pinecones does Jamal have left after giving some away?

  1. 1,807 ✓
  2. 1,817
  3. 1,625
  4. 1,579

Why: To find out how many pinecones Jamal has left, we need to subtract the number of pinecones he gave away from the total he collected. Start with 3,275 pinecones and subtract 1,468: 3,275 - 1,468 = 1,807. Therefore, Jamal has 1,807 pinecones left.

Caleb went to a high school football game in Frisco, Texas, where he enjoyed some nachos and a soda. The nachos cost $4.50, and the soda cost $2.25. How much did Caleb spend in total on nachos and soda at the game?

  1. $6.75 ✓
  2. $7.50
  3. $8.00
  4. $6.25

Why: To find the total amount Caleb spent, add the cost of the nachos ($4.50) and the cost of the soda ($2.25). So, $4.50 + $2.25 = $6.75. Therefore, the correct answer is $6.75.

Nisha is planting flowers in her garden in Austin, Texas. She bought 2 bags of soil to fill her flower bed. Each bag contains 20 liters of soil. How many liters of soil does Nisha have in total?

  1. 40 liters ✓
  2. 30 liters
  3. 20 liters
  4. 50 liters

Why: To find the total amount of soil Nisha has, you need to multiply the number of bags by the amount of soil in each bag. So, 2 bags * 20 liters per bag = 40 liters. Therefore, Nisha has 40 liters of soil in total.

Amara visited the Texas Film Commission in Austin and saw a movie that lasted 2 hours and 25 minutes. If she arrived at the theater at 3:15 PM, what time did the movie end?

  1. 5:40 PM ✓
  2. 5:30 PM
  3. 5:45 PM
  4. 5:55 PM

Why: To find the end time of the movie, first add the duration of the movie (2 hours and 25 minutes) to the start time (3:15 PM). Adding 2 hours to 3:15 PM gives us 5:15 PM. Now, adding 25 minutes to 5:15 PM results in 5:40 PM. Therefore, the movie ended at 5:40 PM.
