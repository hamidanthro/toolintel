Why: To find out how much money Imani collects, multiply the number of cookies sold by the cost of each cookie. So, 15 cookies * $2 = $30. Therefore, the correct answer is $30.

  1. 288 ✓
  2. 300
  3. 250
  4. 360

Why: To find the total cost of charcoal, multiply the number of bags by the cost per bag. Imani needs 36 bags of charcoal at $8 each, so 36 * 8 = 288. Therefore, she will spend a total of $288 on charcoal.

Mario is helping his grandfather on their ranch in Texas. They are repairing a fence that is 245 feet long. If they need to put in 36 fence posts evenly spaced along the length of the fence, how many feet apart will each fence post be from each other?

  1. 6
  2. 7 ✓
  3. 8
  4. 9

Why: To find out how many feet apart each fence post will be, we need to divide the total length of the fence (245 feet) by the number of spaces created by the posts. Since there are 36 posts, this creates 35 spaces. So, we calculate 245 ÷ 35, which equals 7. Therefore, each fence post will be 7 feet apart from each other.

  4. 1300

  1. 3596 ✓

  3. 3580
  4. 3500

Why: To find out how much money Maria spent on the cactus plants, we need to multiply the number of cactus plants (124) by the cost of each cactus ($29). The calculation is 124 * 29. First, we can break it down: 124 * 29 = 124 * (20 + 9) = 124 * 20 + 124 * 9 = 2480 + 1116 = 3596. Thus, the total amount Maria spent is $3596.
