Aanya bought 3 notebooks and 2 pencils for school. Each notebook costs $2.50 and each pencil costs $1.00. How much did Aanya spend in total on the notebooks and pencils?

  1. $9.50 ✓
  2. $8.00
  3. $7.50
  4. $6.00

Why: To find the total cost, first calculate the cost of the notebooks: 3 notebooks * $2.50 = $7.50. Then calculate the cost of the pencils: 2 pencils * $1.00 = $2.00. Finally, add both amounts together: $7.50 + $2.00 = $9.50. Therefore, Aanya spent $9.50 in total.
