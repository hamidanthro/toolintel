Reese is collecting information on different types of fish found in the Gulf of Mexico. He discovers that 1/4 of the fish he sees are redfish, and the remaining fish are speckled trout. If he sees a total of 80 fish, how many speckled trout does Reese see?

  1. 60 ✓
  2. 20
  3. 40
  4. 30

Why: To find the number of speckled trout, first calculate the number of redfish. Since 1/4 of the 80 fish are redfish, Reese sees 80 * 1/4 = 20 redfish. Then, subtract the number of redfish from the total number of fish: 80 - 20 = 60 speckled trout.

Olivia is organizing a picnic in McAllen, Texas, and she wants to prepare some delicious breakfast tacos. Each taco requires 1/4 of a pound of cheese. If she buys a 5-pound bag of cheese, how many tacos can she make?

  1. 20 ✓
  2. 10
  3. 15
  4. 25

Why: To find out how many tacos Olivia can make, divide the total amount of cheese by the amount needed for each taco. Since each taco requires 1/4 pound of cheese, we can calculate the number of tacos by dividing 5 pounds by 1/4. This is the same as multiplying 5 by 4 (because dividing by a fraction is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal). So, 5 * 4 = 20. Therefore, Olivia can make 20 tacos.
