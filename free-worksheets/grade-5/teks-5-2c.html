Elijah is planning a trip from Dallas to the Port of Houston, which is about 56.78 miles away. If Elijah rounds the distance to the nearest tenth, what distance will he round to?

  1. 56.8 ✓
  2. 56.7
  3. 56.0
  4. 57.0

Why: To round 56.78 to the nearest tenth, we look at the hundredths place, which is 8. Since 8 is 5 or greater, we round up the tenths place from 7 to 8. Thus, 56.78 rounded to the nearest tenth is 56.8.

Ximena is helping her family collect oranges from their orchard in the Rio Grande Valley. They collected 36.75 pounds of oranges on Saturday and 22.3 pounds on Sunday. How many pounds of oranges did they collect in total? Round your answer to the nearest tenth.

  1. 59.1 ✓
  2. 59.0
  3. 58.0
  4. 58.5

Why: To find the total weight of oranges collected, add 36.75 pounds and 22.3 pounds. The sum is 59.05 pounds. When rounding 59.05 to the nearest tenth, you look at the hundredths place, which is a 5, so you round up. Therefore, the answer is 59.1 pounds.

  3. 8.4
  4. 8.7

  1. $7.1 ✓
  2. $7.0
  3. $6.9
  4. $7.2

Why: To find the total cost, first multiply the price per pound by the number of pounds: $2.35 * 3 = $7.05. Rounding $7.05 to the nearest tenth, we look at the digit in the hundredths place, which is 5. According to rounding rules, since it's 5 or greater, we round up to $7.1.
