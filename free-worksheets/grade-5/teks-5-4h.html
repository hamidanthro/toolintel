Hana is helping her family build a wooden fence around their backyard in Austin, Texas. The backyard is 30 feet long and 20 feet wide. What is the perimeter of the backyard fence that they need to build?

  1. 100 feet ✓
  2. 60 feet
  3. 50 feet
  4. 80 feet

Why: To find the perimeter of a rectangle, you add together the lengths of all four sides. The formula for perimeter is P = 2 * (length + width). Here, the length is 30 feet and the width is 20 feet. So, P = 2 * (30 + 20) = 2 * 50 = 100 feet. Therefore, the correct answer is 100 feet.
