What makes a tool eligible for review? How do we decide what to review next?

Eligibility Requirements

Documented Policies

Prioritization Framework

We receive more review requests than we can handle. Here's how we decide what to review next:

Factor Priority Why It Matters
High market impact High Tools used by thousands of businesses need scrutiny
Enterprise adoption High Big decisions need independent analysis
Privacy-sensitive use case High Healthcare, legal, finance tools need extra scrutiny
Reader requests Medium We serve our audience first
Emerging category Medium New tool types need early evaluation
Vendor submission Standard Enters queue but no priority boost

Note: Paying the submission fee does not increase priority. It covers our administrative costs and enters you into the standard queue. All tools are evaluated on merit, not payment.
