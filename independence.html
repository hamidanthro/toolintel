Conflict of Interest Protocol

Exactly what happens if a conflict is ever discovered:

Within 24 hours
The affected review is flagged with a yellow banner reading "Independence Under Review"
Within 72 hours
An independent reviewer is identified and notified to conduct a re-evaluation
Within 30 days
The independent reviewer completes a full re-evaluation using the standard methodology
Upon completion
The original review is archived but remains publicly accessible with a disclosure notice. The new independent review is published alongside the original with both clearly labeled.
Permanently
The incident is recorded in the audit trail and in this ledger for complete transparency
Important: A discovered conflict does not mean a review was wrong. It means the process requires an independent second opinion. Both reviews remain public so readers can assess them side by side.
