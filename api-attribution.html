Why Attribution Matters

Our data is independent because we pay to maintain it independently. Attribution ensures buyers know where the intelligence comes from and can verify it at the source.

Core Requirements

  • 1

    Display ToolIntel Name and Link

    All public-facing uses must display the ToolIntel name and link to the source review. This applies to websites, apps, reports, dashboards, and any other medium where ToolIntel data is visible to end users.

  • 2

    No Standalone Resale

    Data may not be resold as a standalone product without an Enterprise license. Building a product that adds value on top of our data is allowed; selling raw exports of our data is not.

  • 3

    No Undisclosed Modifications

    Scores may not be modified or combined with other scores without clear disclosure. If you apply your own weighting or combine ToolIntel scores with other data sources, this must be clearly stated.

  • 4

    Timestamp Historical Data

    Historical data snapshots may not be published without a timestamp and methodology version tag. Scores change over time; users must know when the data was captured and under which methodology.

Publishing Historical Data

"As of February 20, 2026 (ToolIntel Methodology v1.0), Claude scored 89/100"
"Claude scores 89/100" (publishing old data without timestamp)

Creating Composite Scores

"Our VendorRisk score combines ToolIntel's compliance data with our internal risk assessment. ToolIntel compliance score: 91."
Creating a "security score" that blends ToolIntel data with other sources without disclosure

Tier-Specific Requirements

Free Tier: Attribution required. "Powered by ToolIntel" must be visible wherever scores are displayed.

Professional Tier: Attribution required. More flexibility in placement, but source must be clear and linkable.

Enterprise Tier: Custom attribution agreement. May include white-label options with appropriate contractual terms.

Enforcement

We monitor public uses of our API and data. Violations are handled as follows:

First violation: Email notification with 7 days to add attribution.

Second violation: API key suspended pending compliance.

Severe violations: Permanent revocation and potential legal action for commercial misuse.

If you're unsure whether your use case complies, email api@toolintel.ai before launching.
